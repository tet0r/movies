Plot
“Restraint” follows Ron (Travis Fimmel) and Dale (Teresa Palmer) as they run from the authorities following a murder. Along with running for cover, they also take along a hostage. The hostage, Andrew (Stephen Moyer), is an eerie art dealer, who has been living closed up in his home alone, since the disappearance of his fiancée. The three find their way to a beautiful country estate and there they stay. Andrew and Ron become odd companions with a competitive nature, while Andrew has a growing desire for Dale, whom he wants to replace – or become Gabrielle, his former fiancée. Ron and Dale have found themselves in a bad situation - with them fighting for survival against the mounting issues surrounding them and their bad decisions.
