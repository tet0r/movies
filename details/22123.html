Plot
The Legend of Suriyothai is set against the backdrop of political chaos as various areas of the country denounce central leadership and declare their own independent kings. Beautiful Suriyothai (M. L. Piyapas Bhirombhhakdi) is suffering her own personal tragedy and is distraught as she is forced to leave behind her first and true love in order to marry Prince Tien (Sarunyoo Wongkrachang). Her new husband becomes the leading ruler of the country and soon conflict breaks out as a neighbouring country attempts to gain control of the land. It is amongst this backdrop of war that Suriyothai emerges as a heroic figure as she joins the frontline forces and leads an inspired country into battle.
