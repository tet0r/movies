Plot
A Romeo and Juliet story set in a war-torn Japan, the tragedy between the two lovers, Kouga Gennosuke (Joe Odagiri) and Oboro (Yukie Nakama) transcend time as an emotionally epic love story. The Kouga clan and the Oboro clan have produced exceptional samurai for generation, so skilled that they threaten the throne of the high ruler. Perceiving the danger to his rule, Tokugawa Ieyasu (Kazuo Kitamura) deliberately feuls the growing hostility between the two warrior tribes. Although secretly married, Gennosuke and Oboro have no choice but to battle each other. Will their love of each other triumph, or their love of their clan?
