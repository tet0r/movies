Plot
“IP Man 2” is the second story in the incredible tale of IP Man (Donnie Yen), this time centering on his immigration to Hong Kong with his wife, Zhang Yong Cheng (Lynn Hung) in 1949 after World War II. IP Man plans to forward his disciplines of Wing Chun martial arts, a school of his own teachings. He quickly takes on a special student in the form of Wong Shun Leung (Xiaoming Huang). Together they must fight to protect what IP Man has built for himself in Japan when the other martial arts schools want to shut him down. Now, IP Man is forced to fight in triad-like duels against other masters; Lo Meng (Meng Lo) and Fung Hak-On (Hark-On Fung).
