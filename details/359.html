Plot
Two forces emerge as the city sleeps. The forces are invisible but exert strong power over us as we sleep, trying to take our souls through our dreams. The good force gives us hope and strength through our dreams while the evil force fills the subconscious with nightmares full of desperation. John (Chris Kelly) and Emma (Quinn Hunchar) are father and daughter and are in the middle of a dream and forced to fight for John’s soul and save Emma form an everlasting nightmare. They encounter many characters that are scary and unusual, but they only exist in their subconscious...or do they?
