Plot
“Nightmare of Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors” sees the original character from the first film, Nancy Thompson (Heather Langenkamp) become a psychiatrist who specializes in dream therapy. It is here she meets a group of various children who all face the same demon, Freddy Krueger (Robert Englund) who Nancy has some experience with. Together with the help of Kristen (Patricia Arquette), a young girl with the gift of pulling others into her dreams, the kids band together and realize their own special powers in the dream world where they go toe to toe with Freddy himself. But something goes wrong and Freddy kidnaps one of the kids and keeps them in the dream world, now the kids and Nancy along with Max (Larry Fishburne) have to get him back.
