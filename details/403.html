Plot
“Megamind” is the story of the world famous super villain, Megamind (Will Ferrell) who finally is able to defeat and kill his long time nemesis, Metro Man (Brad Pitt). However, Megamind soon learns that every good villain needs an even better good guy nemesis. He becomes bored and uses his own vast intellect to create a new good guy named Tighten (Jonah Hill). Tighten on the other hand has other ideas and sets out to show up Megamind and he becomes a new super villain. Now Megamind in order to hold onto his title of world famous super villain must do something good and stop Tighten, although Megamind learns the perks of being a good guy as he teams with Roxanne Ritchie (Tina Fey) and discovers the power of being the hero.
