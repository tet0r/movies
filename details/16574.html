Plot
Amy Klein (Kari Wuhrer), a London reporter, is sent by her editor Charles Richmond (Simon Kunz) to Bucharest to investigate an underground group. Her editor received footage of the suicide cult that could bring back the dead. Upon arrival, Amy becomes entangled in the group and joins them. She finds one member, Marla (Georgina Rylance), dead in a hotel room at an address she received from her editor. Along with the girl’s corpse, she finds a puzzle box. Amy takes the puzzle box back to her hotel room and, although Marla - the corpse - begged her not to, Amy opens it. This begins a frightening series of events that leads Amy to the cult’s homebase, where the group’s leader, Winter (Paul Rhys), tries to convince her to become a member.
