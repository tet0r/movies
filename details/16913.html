Plot
A collection of all Pixar short subjects released up until 2007, this compilation contains "The Adventures of Andre & Wally B," "Luxo Jr.," Red's Dream," "Tin Toy," "Knick Knack," "Geri's Game," "For The Birds," "Mike's New Car" (with John Goodman and Billy Crystal), Boundin', "Jack-Jack Attack," "One Man Band," "Mater and the Ghostlight" (with Owen Wilson and Larry the Cable Guy), and "Lifted." Also included is the documentary "Pixar Shorts: A Short History," starring the crew of Pixar Studios.
