Plot
During an apocalyptic future, a scientific experiment results in the creation of beings with extraordinary powers, immediately massacred by military units. Only a group survives. The humanoid creatures decide to take revenge on all of humanity with an army of robots. To counter their plans for destruction, responsible for their creation accidental plunges the body of her deceased son in the same liquid solution leading to the birth of the race of mutants. Revenue from the dead, Casshern or Tetsuya Azuma (Yusuke Iseya )is the last hope of mankind. With the help of Professor Azuma Kotaro (Akira Terao) and Midori Azuma (Kanako Higuchi), Casshern will fight for surviving.
