Plot
Battle Royale is a shocking movie where a group of teenaged Japanese classmates are brought to participate in a game on an island. The only catch is that to win the game, and escape from the island, they must kill the rest of their classmates. This haunting story moves quickly as the teens grapple with the psychological terror they are faced with. What will they do? Will they participate in the deadly game? Do they have a choice? Can they find a way off the island without having to kill each other? This fast paced film is a cult classic for a reason.
