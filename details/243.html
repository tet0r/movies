Plot
Sang-hawn (Seung-beom Ryu) is accidently injured by Eui-jin (So-yi Yoon) when the two are in pursuit of the same purse snatching criminal. To make up for her mistake, Eui-jin brings the injured Sang-hwan to her home to be healed by the six Masters of Tao. It is here that the Masters discover that Sang-hwan has the incredible potential to become a great warrior. The earnest police man begins training immediately. Soon all his teachings and training will be tested to defend the entire world.
