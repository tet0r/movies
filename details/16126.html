Plot
Joe Young (Trey Parker) is a young Mormon man, who while knocking on doors looking for souls to save inadvertently; knocks on the door of a pornographer irate at his scene being ruined. Impressed by the way young Joe fights off his henchmen the producer Maxxx Orbison offers him the part or Orgazmo in his movie. Joe lays his morals aside to star in the movie so he can give his fiance her dream wedding, no one he knows will ever find out anyway right? After the movie is released though it becomes a big hit, and his fiance sees it on the shelf of the movie store which causes all kinds of new problems.
