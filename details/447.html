Plot
Comedian Russell Peters (himself) is the star of this comedy show. He specializes in doing many different improvisational skits, situation comedy, expressions, and accents. He is performing primarily in San Francisco, doing comedy that skewers every ethnic group and individual, including himself. The audience itself is of mixed races and ethnic groups. His brand of comedy is politically incorrect and is not meant for anyone who is easily offended or very sensitive. Also, some of the humor is intended only for those who are over the age of eighteen. He especially likes to do bits about those from India and China due to his own background.
