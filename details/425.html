Plot
It's not your ordinary first day at the office when The Fool (Joe Anderson) begins working for Omega, a secret team of spies run by the U. S. government. They, along with rival team Alpha, do the kind of black-ops missions you never hear about, and don't want to think about. But when the boss turns up dead, things go south quickly. With mentor Chariot (Rob Corddry) and former love Temperance (Odette Yustman), he must figure out who he can trust and who's trying to kill him. With the building about to explode, and assassins behind every door, their chief concern is simply to get out alive. Maybe the mysterious Hermit (Zach Galifianakis) knows the way. It's a race against time, and nothing is as it seems. It's Operation Endgame.
