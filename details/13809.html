Plot
Toxic Avenger 3: Last Temptation Of Toxie is the third film in the popular film series, The Toxic Avenger. In this movie, The Toxic Avenger (Ron Fazio and John Altamura) has recently finished completely ridding his once corrupt city of Troma of all its criminals and murders. Now, with nothing to do and no criminals to thwart, he finds his days extremely monotonous and struggles greatly with a lack of purpose in his life. When he is offered a position at the once evil corporation, Apocalypse Incorporated, he takes it. While working there he discovers that his duties as the Toxic Avenger are far from complete and he must once again channel his great powers.
