Plot
This mock documentary features Buford Lee (James Lew), who wants to launch a new martial arts film called 18 Fingers of Death. A documentary filmmaker and fan, Ronald Mack (Maurice Patten) covers his attempts and problems. There are spoofs on such heroes as Stephen Seafood (Kirk Graves), Antonio Bandana (Lorenzo Lamas), and Chuck Snorris (Charlie George Brown). There is also a long fight scene between Lee and Bandana involving a great deal of flatulence. Lee also enjoys showing Mack a collection of cherished Kung Fu items, which feature Bruce Lee’s dirty socks that he had worn in the film Enter the Dragon.
