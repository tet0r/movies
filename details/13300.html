Plot
Tina (Pressly), Christie (Valance), Kasumi (Aoki), and Helena (Carter) are four accomplished martial artists whose externally sexy looks deceives the fact, the deadly nature. When the fearsome females each receive an invitation to play a part in an illegal martial arts contest to be staged on a distant Asian island, the vision of a ten-million-dollar top prize is only sweetened by the reality that they will be facing off in opposition to some of the toughest fighters on the earth. Now, in order to protect their respect and look for the answers to life's greatest mysteries, Tina, Christie, Kasumi, and Helena will check their reserves at the beach and get ready for a series of fights that will check not only their strength, endurance, and determination, but the very foundation of their dedication to the martial arts as well.
