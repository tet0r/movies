Plot
An aging cop Detective Chan Kwok Chung (Simon Yam) is deparate to capture a notorious gangster Wong Po (Sammo Hung) who was responsible for the death of his goddaughter. His bad health makes the job that much harder and he resorts to not so ethical means to accomplish his goal. To complicate his plight even more, the sudden demise of an agent he is working with makes it necessary to bring on new blood. an ethical detective Ma Kwun (Donnie Yen). Will Chung be able to complete his mission despite his bad health and the roadblocks he faces along the way?
