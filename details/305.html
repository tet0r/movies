Plot
A group of eight Norwegian medical students decide to go away for an Easter ski vacation to release their stress from school at one of their friend's remote cabins up in the mountains. Upon their arrival, the students noticed that Sara (Ane Dahl Torp) has not arrived yet, since she is the one who has traveled ahead of them, yet at the same time unwitting to them the area which they are staying for the night was once used by the Nazis during the German occupation in Norway in World War II. During the war, the Nazis have made the neighboring resident's lives a living hell but somehow the residents managed to drive the Nazis up to the cold mountain where they later die of freezing. When the students accidentally stumbled upon a hidden box which contains gold, it causes the menacing Nazi zombies to come back in order to reclaim their treasure for which the students are entirely not well prepared for.
