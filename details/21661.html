Plot
Cheongpung myeongwol(Sword in the Moon) is a South Korean movie. In this movie,the main characters Yoon Gyu-yup(Cho jae-hyun) and Choi Ji-hwan(Choi Min-su), are two best and inseparable friends who are also the best swordsmen. In order to maintain peace,harmony and safety in the country, A military unit of elite is created at the end of the Joseon Dynasty. Gyu-yup and Ji-hwan are best friends but the circumstances of the Gyu-yup got changed which along with the political plot forces Gyu-yup to kill his friend Choi Ji-hwan and his fencing master. Beacause of the circumstances,Gyu-yup becomes cruel and cold blooded soldier. He was called the human butcher. Then it enquires into a series of political assassinations which aimed at the protagonists of the plot. At the end,Gyu-yup discovered a sword which carried the seal of elite from this sword Gyu-yup discovered that Ji-hwan was back and he was alive.
