Plot
"Hobo With A Shotgun" is the story of a man simply known as Hobo (Rutger Hauer) who arrives in a new city to try and find peace and what little comforts a homeless man can find. What Hobo finds is a city torn apart. As he makes friends with a young man named Slick (Gregory Smith), Hobo discovers the authorities of the city have their hands tied. Chief Wakeum (Jeremy Akerman) doesn’t know who to trust in his prescient as his men are bribed and corrupted. But Hobo takes his fight to the source as he grabs a trusty shotgun and heads to see the Gang Leader (Glen Matthews) himself.
