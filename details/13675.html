Plot
Queen Taramis (Sarah Doublas) hires the famous adventurer Conan (Arnold Schwarzenegger) to go rescue the kidnapped princess Jenna (Olivia D'abo) and acquire a mystical crystal. Conan, along with companions Bombaata (Wilt Chamberlain), warrior woman Zula (Grace Jones), thief Malak (Tracey Walter) and wizard Akiro (Mako) head off to fight the evil wizard and save the beauty. But when they get back they find that the queen has nefarious plans of her own for the crystal and the poor princess. Betrayed and overwhelmed Conan must not only keep the princess safe but somehow stop a demonic god that will destroy the world.
