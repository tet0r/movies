Plot
For Amy Blue (Rose McGowan) and her boyfriend Jordan White (James Duval), life is nothing but crazy days of drugs and almost sex. Although Amy is ready, Jordan just can’t get over his fear of contracting AIDS so he resists her every advance. They decide to take off one day and embark on an adventuresome road trip. They see a hitchhiker and pick him up. What they don’t know is that Xavier Red (Johnathon Schaech) has a hobby of killing people. Together the three of them head across the country on a spree of drugs and killing. The sexual tension rises though, when X and Amy begin having sex. Jordan must make the choice of joining in or watching his girl with another man. To make matters worse, someone is looking for the kids who keep killing people.
