Plot
A battle for power in this animated film takes place when Lex Luthor (Clancy Brown) somehow becomes president and matches Superman (Tim Daly) against Batman (Kevin Conroy). Lex has managed to strip them of their hero status and they are now looked upon as outcast and ex-heroes who cannot be trusted at all. Will Batman and Superman remain on the dark side or will they be able to return to their super hero status. The battle is on and Lex Luthor seems to be getting his way. Who can turn the tables and save the world from Luthor’ plans.
