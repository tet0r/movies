Plot
When the famous super-hero Captain Amazing (Greg Kinnear) is kidnapped by the nefarious Casanova Frankenstein (Geoffrey Rush) and Champion City is left without a protector seven “mysterious” wannabe heroes catch wind of Casanova’s latest deed, and step into the spotlight to save the day. Lead by Mr. Furious (Ben Stiller), Blue Raja (Hank Azaria) and The Shoveler (William H. Macy) the Mystery Men are formed of a group of seven unique heroes all gifted with what some would call “incredible” abilities. Together they hatch a plan to save rescue Captain Amazing from Casanova Frankenstein and prevent his rain of terror from taking over the city.
