Plot
"Emmanuelle In Hong Kong" is the story about Yan (Akeno Junko) and Emily (Grace Lam) who for years have had a less than decent sex life. Emily has been a devoted Catholic who always saw sex as the damnable act she was raised to believe it to be. This drive her husband, Yan to the edge of madness in which he arranges for his wife to be kidnapped. She is taken to a sexual den for three months where Emily is taught a thing or two about sex and the marital bed by some of the sexual vixens under the den‘s employ.
