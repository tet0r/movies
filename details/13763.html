Plot
It starts out as just another ordinary day at a New Jersey convenience store as Dante (Brian O’Halloran) and Randal (Jeff Anderson) sell cigarettes, rent out videos and deal with a host of increasingly bizarre customers. But Dante soon faces a choice between his current steady girlfriend Veronica or his old high school flame Caitlin. Dante must sort out his romantic woes while suffering through another trying day at work, all the while venting to Randal about his fears, concerns, and opinions on the “Star Wars” trilogy. When Dante finally decides which woman to choose, the resulting chaos ensures that this will prove anything but another ordinary day behind the counter.
