Plot
“Masters of the Universe” sees the planet Eternia, torn apart by war and ruled by the evil Skeletor (Frank Langella). The tyrant has everything but the secrets of castle Greyskull. So he has his men collect the key maker, Gwildor (Billy Barty) however He-Man (Dolph Lundgren) and his freedom fighters, Teela (Chelsea Field) and Man-at-Arms (Jon Cypher) are there to stop the abduction. In a battle the four are accidentally transported to Earth where they loose the key, a device Gwildor has constructed and what Skeletor wants. The key has landed into the hands of two humans, Julie Winston (Courteney Cox) and Kevin Corrigan (Robert Duncan McNeill). Now Skeletor sends his own forces to Earth led by the vile Evil-Lyn (Meg Foster). Now a battle that began half way across the universe will be finished on Earth.
