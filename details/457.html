Plot
The human colony from planet Sirius 6B was completely destroyed thirteen years ago after victims were hacked viciously into pieces by robotic killing machines known as Screamers. Now a group of rescuers are sent to investigate a distress signal which originated from an abandoned planet. Their mission to rescue any remaining survivors becomes difficult as they have less than a week to get it done as the planet is potentially in danger of an incoming solar storm. But their biggest surprise is when they discover that the killing machines have turned into mutants that are more powerful than ever before.
