Plot
“Lust for Dracula” is a modern telling of the classic story Dracula. It centers on Mina Harker (Misty Mundae) a young woman just recently wed to the Hollywood pharmaceuticals giant, Jonathan Harker (Julian Wells). Mina wants nothing more than to have a family but Jonathan is far too busy. A rival pharmacologist comes to America, the Countess Dracula (Darian Caine). The Countess takes a desired liking to Mina and turns her into a vampire. Now Sara (Andrea Davis), Mina’s sister has to get not only Jonathan’s attentions for help but also the help of the mysterious Abigal Van Helsing (Shelly Jones), together the group hunt down this Countess for her ultimate true death.
