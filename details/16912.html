Plot
Jason Voorhees and Freddy Kruger clash in this crossover classic. Once again in the town haunted by the evil spirit that terrorizes children in thier dreams, Freddy enlists the help of Jason to terrorize and ruthlessly take revenge on the local teen population. Jason runs them down them in the waking world, while Freddy gives them the axe in the dream world. Fortunately, the teens discover that something is amiss, and investigate the town hall to uncover a dark secret. They must solve the riddle of Elm Street, all the while running from a machete wielding madman and his dream-weaving accomplice. It's not long until Jason wants the teens for himself, and the final showdown between two of the most feared supernatural horrors begins...
