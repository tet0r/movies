Plot
“Hellraiser 5: Inferno” is yet again another adventure of the demonic puzzle box from hell. A shady police detective, Detective Joseph Thorne (Craig Sheffer) who is on the take and tampers with evidence comes across the fabled puzzle box. As Thorne tries to leave the box behind it keeps returning and when he’s placed on a case to find someone known as ‘The Engineer’ a figure he comes to never meet but hear so much about. Thorne’s investigation takes shim deeper into a strange world of murder, sadism and utter madness as he encounters the demonic Pinhead (Doug Bradley) who shows Thorne that there is no redemption for one such as him.
