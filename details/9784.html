Plot
The very brave hero Prince Talon (Lee Horsley) is tasked with protecting his father, the King against a sinister rebellion in their kingdom. Armed with a mysterious three-pronged sword he leads a group of soldiers to quash this rebellion, but finds that the enemy is quite an opposition for him and his unit. They fight valiantly, as many on both sides perish in the battle and the Prince now makes his way to find his father and end the evil threat once and for all, which will save the kingdom. His efforts though, will be met with opposition which he must not estimate or they will all die.
