Plot
Sky high is a high school for potential super heroes and their sidekicks. These teens have super strength, the ability to fly, become invisible or inflate them selves among other things. This automatic placement strategy makes an already awkward high school introduction even more distressing for Will Stronghold (Michael Angarano). Will is the son of two world renowned heroes, The Commander (Kurt Russell) and Jetstream (Kelly Preston). He’s also a late bloomer with no super powers yet. Will is branded as a sidekick as a result. Will’s freshman high school experience becomes one of legendary proportions as he tackles girl problems, numerous bullies, snobbish heroes and a dreadful gym teacher. There’s also an evil villain in the mix that’s after the Commander, Jetstream and all of the other potential heroes and their families. Will must use his wits and his new comrades to bring down the villainous ‘heroes’, if only he had super powers.
