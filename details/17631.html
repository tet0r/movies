Plot
The main character in the story is young Pierre (Louis Garrel) who had left a catholic school to live with his parents. His father (Philipe Duclos) dies early in the movie,by telling his mother, Hélène (Isabelle Huppert) to care for Pierre. After that Pierre learn that his mother has been unfaithful to her husband many times which was revealed by herself. Even she wants her son in the same way. After that she asks Rea (Joana Preiss), to take her son's virginity. she lefts her son alone and goes out to travel. After her departure Hansi (Emma de Caunes) enters into his life as a friend. she returns after a exhausted adventures, and saw Pierre and Hansi in a bar and greets each other,by looking into each other eyes. she asks her son to sleep with her to night. And finally she commits suicide for what she had done.
