Plot
After the first Azumi movie, Azumi (Aya Ueto) and her closest friend Nagara (Yuma Ishigaki) are being hunted for the assassinations that they carried out earlier. They have a battle with a ninja and then retreat to a temple so Nagara can heal. While this is going on, the enemy, Kanbei (Kazuki Kitamura), is assembling a large army of mercenaries and bandits to try to capture the pair of assassins. Now Azumi is after her final target, Masayuki Sanada (Mikijiro Hira). During the quest Nigara is killed and Azumi meets another enemy, Sandada, and accepts his duel offer out of deep sadness caused by the loss of her loved ones. Azumi wins the battle and leaves to live on her own.
