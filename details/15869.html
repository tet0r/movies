Plot
Roses daughter Sharon is having horrible bouts with sleepwalking and she always mentions the town of Silent Hill. Rose decides to take Sharon to this ghost town to figure out why she keeps having dreams about it. The journey to the truth brings more questions as Rose digs deeper into the mystery of Silent Hill. She realizes that this isn't your normal town. Every night something happens, something unnatural and evil. She meets with people who she thinks are trustworthy, but are they? She can only trust herself as she must fight to save her life as well as Sharons.
