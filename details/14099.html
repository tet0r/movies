Plot
With a backdrop of fighting superpower, several men and women fight brutally to the death because of their jealousy and unrequited love for each other. Meng Sing Wan (Tony Leung) may be in love with Butterfly (Joey Wang), or it may be a ruse to throw off the evil kung fun mistress named Sister Ko (Michelle Yeoh) whom he seems to treat as a blood relative, but she has the power to rip off the heads of her enemies. Deception is a way of life to protect oneself from those who may have greater super powers. Meng Sing Wan gets Butterfly pregnant, but the reaction of Sister Ko seems far more envious and upset than a sibling ought to be. Whatever the feelings of the kung fu masters, their duty is to fight the evil Master Suen (Elvis Tsui) whose group wants to kill all others to become the controlling force of the world.
