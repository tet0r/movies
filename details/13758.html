Plot
Dante (Brian O'Halloran), Randal (Jeff Anderson), Jay (Jason Mewes), and Silent Bob (Kevin Smith), the major figures of the independent black and white film "Clerks" are back in color and animated form. Unlike the film, our heroes are opposed by ruthless billionaire Leonardo Lenonardo (Alec Baldwin), and visited by a bevy of guest stars such as Gwyneth Paltrow, James Woods and Julia Sweeny through a half-dozen rapid-fire gag filled adventures. These stories range from a parody of television conventions such as "the flashback show" to parodies of films. Some of these include: "The Last Star Fighter," "Indiana Jones," "Outbreak," "Fast Times at Ridgemont High," and virtually every courtroom movie ever made.
