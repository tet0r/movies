Plot
Taking place some time after the first film, “Day Watch” sees what happens when the sun comes up. Set in the backdrop of vampires, werewolves and demons, Anton (Konstantin Khabensky) finds himself still in search of the truth behind what the forces of Darkness desire. On top of this, Anton is still seeking his son who is missing and presumed abducted by the same people behind such forces. However on his quest, Dark Others start appearing slain all across the city and all evidence leads to Anton who is now on the run from his own people. He escaped the Night Watch now Anton must face the Day Watch.
