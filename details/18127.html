Plot
Park Hee-bong is the loving father of three children. They live on the bank of Seoul's Han River, where Park runs a small snackbar. His daughter, Hyun-seo, is the favorite of her father and her brothers. Her oldest brother is immature and selfish, and her middle brother is a competitive athlete. But when a strange mutant invades the river and starts killing people, the whole area is concerned. And when Hyun-seo is taken, the three men are in totally disbelief. They soon realize Hyun-seo is still alive and they must work together to save her. But will they find her in time?
