Plot
“Once Upon A Time In China" is a film set in the late 19th Century in Canton. It focuses on the life of the legendary martial arts hero Wong- Fei-Hung (Jet Li) who made a stance against foreign forces who were plundering China after the Second World War. Fei-Hung becomes a bit more discouraged when his Aunt Yee (Rosamund Kwan) returns from the United States completely assimilated into Western Culture he assumes the role of her personal guardian. His relationship with his aunt becomes even more strained when Fei-Hung’s martial arts school and the local military militia become involved in ever increasing conflicts with American, French and local governments. As the battles rage and violence ensues even Aunt Yee has to question her new western views and Fei-Hung proves that you can fight guns with Kung Fu.
