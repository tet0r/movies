Plot
Kim Kyung-Soo (Jang Hyuk) is a troubled young man. He gets in fights. He steals. He has no respect for authority. With only one place left to turn, his parents send him to Volcano High. This isn’t an ordinary high school. The only students who are accepted are those who have an outstanding skill in one of the martial arts. Kim is finally looking forward to going to school because he absolutely loves and is quite talented in martial arts. However, more than martial arts awaits him. Volcano High also demands that its students learn self-control and adhere to a strict code of behavior. This will be the true challenge that Kim will face if he is to finally succeed.
