Plot
When the football team of Texas State University are sacked for corruption, the replacements must come from the actual students without using scholarship places. Coach Ed Gennero (Hector Elizondo) fields a team with less numbers than other teams but includes Paul Blake (Scott Bakula) a quarterback who has returned to school after dropping out sixteen years ago when his father died. Paul feels uncomfortable about playing football as an older student and also needs to figure out how to romance Dr Suzanne Carter (Harley Jane Kozak) his journalism professor. Playing ironman football the team must learn to rely on each other to overcome their problems and become a team of champions.
