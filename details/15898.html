Plot
When Steve Barker (Johnny Knoxville) becomes down on his luck, he still feels indebted to a foreign man named Stavi (Luis Avalos). But when Stavi while under his watch becomes injured he feels compelled to call on some old debts to help. Unknown to him the enjoyment that his deadbeat uncle Gary Barker (Brian Cox) would have in store for him. Namely, pretending to be a handicap man named Jeffy in order to rig the special Olympics to win its cash prize. Within this insane idea, Steve finds friendship, love and a profound understanding that even the handicap can still throw a few good insults and more so punches.
