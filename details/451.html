Plot
Former gambling addict John Alighieri (Steve Buscemi) kisses his old habit goodbye and moves from Vegas to Albuquerque to distance himself from the temptations. He can't drop the game altogether, though, and his new vice of scratch tickets is leaving him financially strapped, in spite of his job as an insurance claims adjuster. Things are finally looking up for John when his boss, Mr. Townsend (Peter Dinklage), offers him a chance to work in the fraud department along with stoic and cynical Virgil (Romany Malco). Their mission? Prove that Vegas stripper Tasty D. Lite (Emmanuelle Chirqui) is faking her reported injuries. Can John return to Vegas without rejoining his old gambling habits, and find the proof he needs to do his job, or will his temptations be too strong?
