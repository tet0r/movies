Plot
Captain Kirk (William Shatner) and the crew of the USS Enterprise, Dr. “Bones” McCoy (DeForest Kelley), Sulu (George Takei), Chekhov (Walter Koenig), Scotty (James Doohan), and Uhura (Nichelle Nichols) return to the planet Genesis to retrieve the body of Spock (Leonard Nimoy) who sacrificed himself in order to save the Enterprise from Khan. However, Klingons are also racing to the planet to discover its hidden secrets and the powerful weapon that helped create it. Expedition teams from both the Starfleet Federation and the Klingon Empire race to discover the secrets of the planet in this third installment of the Star Trek franchise.
