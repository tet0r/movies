Plot
A sadistic individual, who the Police have nicknamed jigsaw, disgusted with the morals of society and the way people take their life for granted, takes it upon himself to teach his victims the value of life. Once abducted his victims are forced to make unbelievable choices in order to try to save their lives and win their freedom. no one is safe from jigsaw's sadistic games. From police officers (Danny Glover) to drug addicts (Monica Potter) to well respected doctors (Cary Elwes) everyone is fair game.
