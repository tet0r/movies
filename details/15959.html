Plot
“Billy Jack” is the story about Billy Jack (Tom Laughlin) a half-Indian/ half-white ex-Green Beret who is slowly discovering his Indian heritage more and more. He loathes violence but seems to never be able to escape such things in the white man’s world. All around Billy Jack sees nothing but anti-establishments, the senseless slaughter of God’s creatures and the rape and pillage of society by a racial segregated and two sides judicial system. Now students of a peace loving free arts school are pitted against the bad guys of a local town with Billy Jack caught right in the middle.
