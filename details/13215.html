Plot
The Pink Floyd PULSE concert film features original Floyd members David Gilmour, Nick Mason, Richard Wright with Guy Pratt on bass and Dick Parry on saxophone and horns. The concert was videotaped on October 20, 1994 at Earls Court in London. This concert film shows the band’s tour following the release of the Division Bell album. The movie features many of Pink Floyd's greatest hits including Wish You Were Here, Shine On You Crazy Diamond, Dark Side Of The Moon (in its entirety) as well as many songs off the then new PULSE album. The bands signature round video screen is back with new images to accompany many of the songs. With no production expense spared, this is the ultimate Pink Floyd concert and shows their commitment to using lights and effects to complement and enhance their music.
