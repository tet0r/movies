Plot
Kum Lhau (Petchtai Wongkamlao) is a secret service agent of the republic of Wongnaileum and is sent to a mission to Bangkok to unearth the major Terrorist activities. In order to accomplish his mission, he starts to work as a luk thung singer for a record company. Later he discovers that the company for which he is working is itself engaged in business for providing weapons of mass destruction to the gang of terrorists. During his course of investigation, he discovers that the chief executive of the record company Paula (Jacqueline Apitananon) is also on the same mission and is a CIA agent. They together work to unearth and stop the deadly act of exploding the town. During this course Kum Lhau's wife Keaw (Janet Keaw) discovers that Kum Lhau has lied to her about his job as a secret service agent and that he is working as a labor in Thailand.
