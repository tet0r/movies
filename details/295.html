Plot
"Superman/Shazam!: The Return Of The Black Adam" is the adventurous tale of Black Adam’s (Arnold Vosloo) return to the land of the living after a 5,000 year exile in the land of the dead. The once deceased Egyptian god has returned to wreck havoc on the land of the living. Black Adam is causing all this chaos to call out his mortal enemy Shazam (James Garner) or more widely known as Captain Marvel. Superman (George Newbern) arrives on the scene and finds Black Adam only to be defeated by the magical God. Now, young Billy Batson (Zach Callison) must overcome his fears to become Shazam once more to defeat Black Adam.
