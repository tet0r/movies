Plot
A contaminated soft drink is the cause of a zombie infection in a shopping mall. Two goofy ne'er-do-well buddies with big dreams of success and love, Woody Invincible (Siu Chun Chan) and Crazy Bee (Chan Sum Lee), are salesmen in the mall. One night before the mall closes. Woody and Crazy Bee are commanded by their boss to take his car and run a personal errand for him. Returning they accidentally hit a pedestrian walking in the street. Believing it‘s their fault, they stuff is body in the trunk of the car and return to work, parking the car in the mall‘s parking garage. Hoping that they can figure a way out of the mess by the time the mall closes Unwittingly, Woody and Crazy Bee have more on their hands than they know.
