Plot
Critters 2: The Main Course is the second installment in the Critters series of movies. In this film the Critters return from space and land in the small, quiet town of Grovers Bend. The town folks unaware of their presence at first mistake them for Easter eggs, a mistake that later proves fatal for many of the towns residents. Their only hope for survival is a group of resilient bounty hunters (Don Opper and Terrence Mann) who prove a worthy match for the ferociously terrifying critters. Only the bounty hunters’ wit and tenacity will allow them to prevail and rescue the town from the unrelenting Critters.
