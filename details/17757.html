Plot
Yi Kwak (Woo-sung Jung), a soldier of the imperial guard, has the ability to see ghosts. The guards receive the mission to defeat the demons which assault the palace and all the soldiers except Yi Kwak, are killed. While hiding in a temple, the hero comes across the entrance of the Purgatory, where souls wait for 49 days before they reincarnate. Here he meets So Hwa (Tae-hee Kim), a goddess who looks very much like his dead girlfriend. So Hwa guards the gate between the Purgatory and the land of the living and is threatened by an army of souls who want to return on Earth. Yi Kwak is eager to help her only to discover that the army is led by his former colleagues from the imperial guard.
