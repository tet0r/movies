Plot
Jennifer Hills (Sarah Butler) is a writer longing for an overdue vacation. Taking a retreat into the woods to not only rest, but to also to work on her next book, Jennifer finds more than she bargained for. Her presence in town has upset the locals who venture to her cabin one night to scare her into leaving. However Johnny (Jeff Branson), Storch (Andrew Howard), Stanley (Daniel Franzese) and Andy (Rodney Eastman) let things get out of hand as their scare tactic turns into hours of sexual assaults. Jennifer finally escapes and seems to fall to her death in a raging river. The attackers believe her to be dead. Yet she survives and returns, looking for her pound of flesh.
