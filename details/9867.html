Plot
Ben Thomas (Will Smith) is on a mission to drastically change the lives of 7 special individuals. But first he needs to make sure they deserve these gifts he’s about to bestow. On his journey he comes across Ezra Turner (Woody Harrelson) a blind man, Emily Posa (Rosario Dawson) a woman with a deadly heart disease in need of a heart transplant, and several others also in need of a miracle. Throughout, Ben has constant flash backs of his wife and a car accident. He also has frequent calls of his brother wondering where he is at and what is he doing. Can these people really trust Ben Thomas and what are his motives?
