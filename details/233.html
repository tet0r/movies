Plot
An unknown band comprised of Chazz (Brendan Fraser), Rex (Steve Buscemi), and Pip (Adam Sandler) try to hijack a radio station in the hopes of getting found. Unfortunately they do get found, most by the police. Two of the finest to arrest these heavy metal band gone wild, are Office Wilson (Chris Farley) and Sergeant O’Malley (Ernie Hudson). This fun and light-hearted comedy is an enjoyable night from beginning to end. All Chazz wants to do is become famous and impress her rock and roll style soon to be ex-girlfriend. Will they land on the big stage or will they land in the big house. You will enjoy the ride.
