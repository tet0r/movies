Plot
The members of an elite U.S. Special Forces are sent on a extremely dangerous mission to "search and destroy" in Bolivia. The team featuring Clay (Jeffrey Dean Morgan), Roque (Idris Elba), Jensen (Chris Evans), Pooch (Columbus Short) and Cougar (Oscar Jaenada) gets betrayed by no else than their superior, a shameless boss called Max (Jason Patric). The team must work together to reveal that they're not dead to the world in one of the most treacherous environments in the world, the Bolivian jungle. They get a special backup from a mysterious but gorgeous woman called Aisha. A battle featuring high-tech gadgets, powerful weapons and amazing action scenes begins.
