Plot
In order to search for his nemesis, Preest (Ryan Phillippe) masks himself as a vigilante detective roaming the streets of Meanwhile City: a monolithic fantasy metropolis, ruthlessly governed by religious and faith. Esser (Bernard Hill) is a deeply religious man who arrives in London trying to locate his wayward son who has escaped from psychiatric care. Whereas Milo (Sam Riley) is devastated after being dumped at the altar and wonders if he will ever find the one for him. In the mean time beautiful art student Emilia (Eva Green), who is born in a privileged family, gets forced to endure therapy with her mother, who she despises. In the course of time it gets obvious that these persons share a similar fate.
