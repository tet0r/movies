Plot
Stan (Trey Parker), Kyle (Matt Stone), and Butters (Matt Stone) are in Imaginationland, but find that their visit there is fairly bad timing. Terrorists have attacked the land, and their unique attack has seen some of the most evil and scary characters on the loose. With seemingly no other alternative, the government prepares to nuke Imaginationland in order to save the world from the imaginations that are running out of control. In their desperation, the people of Imaginationland find that their hope lies on the shoulders of one: Butters. With all of the residents relying on Butters, will he step up and find a way to save them all?
