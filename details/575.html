Plot
‘Versus’ is set in the present day where two escaped prisoners (Tak Sakaguchi and Hideo Sakaki) reach a remote forest and meet with a group of gangsters called the Yakuza. Unknown to the prisoners the forest that they have reached is the Forest of Resurrection and is a portal or gateway to the other side. The prisoners and the gangsters get embroiled in a dispute about a girl that was kidnapped by the gangsters and during the argument one of the gangsters is killed. The forest becomes an even more disturbing place to be as the bodies of those once dead come back to life. An evil spirit is also resurrected and the fight becomes a very intense battle between good against evil.
