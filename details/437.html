Plot
Credit card purchases for transplants of body organs result in some non-payment problems in the future when accounts fall into the arrears. When a customer falls behind in payment, Remy (Jude Law) and Jake (Forest Whitaker) are called in by creditors to repossess the heart, liver, or whatever has been purchased. Frank (Live Schreiber) thinks they are the best at their quick ambulance work, taking organs almost immediately after the delinquent customer is put under anesthesia. Though Remy is tops at cutting, he has lost his heart for the job and helps fugitive Beth (Alice Braga) go on the run to keep from being relieved of her transplant. His wife Carol (Carice van Houten) has also told him to lose the career or lose her.
