Plot
Lucas (Corey Haim) is a nerdy, lonely high school student who becomes friends with the new girl in town, Maggie (Kerri Green) over the summer. However, when school starts, Maggie falls in with the cool crowd and falls for the star football player, Cap (Charlie Sheen). Lucas is so obsessed with Maggie and tries so hard to find a place in her new social circle, he neglects the attentions of the mousy Rena (Winona Ryder). Lucas, small and unathletic, joins the football team in an effort to ingratiate himself with the popular clique. He is sidelined for most of the season, but when the coach puts him in during the climactic game, Lucas has a chance to win the heart of Maggie after all.
