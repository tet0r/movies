Plot
In the legendary kingdom of Zu, a group of immortal warriors - King Sky (Ekin Cheng,) Red (Louis Koo) and White Eyebrows (Sammo Hung Kam-Bo) - team up to fight an unspeakable evil that could destroy the world. They try a series of bizarre, daring and elaborate plans, but each one ends in failure. Along the way they meet up with various unusual characters. Joy (Ziyi Zhang) is a mortal girl who joins their fight, while Enigma (Cecilia Cheung) is somebody that King Sky thought he'd never see again.
