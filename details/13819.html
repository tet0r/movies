Plot
Citizen Toxie: The Toxic Avenger IV is the forth chapter of the popular film series and concludes the incredible story of the Toxic Avenger (David Mattey and Clyde Lewis). In this chapter, the evil criminal organization known as the Diaper Mob has taken hostage many disabled students at a local Troma school. Now the authorities call on the Toxic Avenger and a rag tag group of various other superheroes and protagonists to save the kids. On his way, the Toxic Avenger falls into a strange time rift where his evil twin ends up in Troma and he is in an alternate dimension. Now he must race against time to save the school and the city against his dangerous counterpart before it is too late.
