Plot
“Silver Hawk” is an action adventure that centers on both Lulu Wong (Michelle Yeoh) and Akira Shiraishi (Kouichi Iwaki), two childhood friends. One, Akira grows up to become a police officer while Lulu follows another path and becomes the costumed vigilante known as Silver Hawk. The intrigue begins when Professor Ho Chung (Daming Chen) is kidnapped by millionaire, Alexander Wolfe (Luke Goss) who has a plan for world domination revolving around a small technological item just about everyone has in their possession; a cellular phone. Now Silver Hawk, Akira and Kit (Brandon Chang), Silver Hawk’s young side kick must find the Professor, stop Wolfe and save the world.
