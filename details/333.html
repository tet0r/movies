Plot
Hell Ride is a story about a biker gang called The Victors and their notorious leader's, Pistolero (Larry Bishop), inner-demons and vengeance against his long-lost lover Cherokee Kizum's (Julia Jones) killers. Not only that, but the rival gang, headed by The Deuce (David Carradine) and Billy-Wing (Vinnie Jones), are coming to shed The Victor's blood. With Pisterolo's crew being slaughtered and turning ship against him, he is only left with his two long-time gangmembers The Gent (Michael Madsen) and Eddie Zero (Dennis Hopper), as well as his new "Left Wing" Commanche (Eric Balfour), to stop the coming onslaught of Billy-Wing and his Victor-hungry gang. Hell Ride was directed and written by Larry Bishop.
