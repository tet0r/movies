Written as a proof of concept as well as stand-alone prologue to forthcoming horror feature "Reunion", Guardians tells the story of a young man who is moving into an abandoned school as a live-in guardian. One drizzly night, he meets estate agent Helen, who shows him into the school, leading them both into a terrifying trap.
