Plot
Kelly (Lucinda Dickey) is a young dancer who has gotten into the breakdancing scene thanks to her friends Ozone (Adolfo Quinones) and Turbo (Michael Chambers). When a greedy developer makes plans to bulldoze the local community recreation center in order to put up a condominium development, the trio band together to raise awareness and funds to pay off the center's mortgage.
