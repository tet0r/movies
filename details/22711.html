Plot
Godzilla 2000 sees the return of Japan’s city stomping monster. The King of all Monsters is faced with an all-new menace in this relaunch of the classic Japanese film franchise. This film ignores the other Godzilla movies with the exception of the first one. It begins with Godzilla having already attacked Tokyo and is returning to the sea. Meanwhile a large, ancient rock is discovered by Prof. Yuji Shinoda (Takehiro Murata) who finds to his surprise that it floats and flies off. The rock has chased down Godzilla and when the lizard attacks the rock it breaks open revealing a UFO. The UFO needs a strong type of DNA to regenerate a body for itself here on Earth and it has set it’s sights on Godzilla. The UFO puts up a huge fight and is finally defeated. Just when Godzilla believes his fight is over, the UFO morphs into a vicious monster named Orga. The two gigantic monsters battle it out with all of Japan as their battlefield.
