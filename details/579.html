Plot
Ben (Robert De Niro) is a Hollywood producer who doesn’t have things going his way. He is trying to fix Sean Penn’s latest film called Fiercely, which is supposed to have its world premiere at Cannes. There are many problems with Mr. Penn’s film most notably the ending which involves a dog and a death. Many A- list stars make appearances, including the already mentioned Sean Penn and Bruce Willis as the lead actor in Fiercely. What Just Happened? is interested in showing the craziness of making a film in Hollywood. No aspect of Hollywood is safe from being made fun of although most of the satire is in good fun.
