Plot
Thirty-something, but an aging fan of old television shows, a young filmmaker has great trivia knowledge of movies. Mark (Eric McCormack) tries to create homage to the past of television by making a bad independent movie called “Bradykillers” who torment girls named after the famous sisters. With his friend Robert (Rafer Weigel), they lead a shadowy life, wishing to be involved in movies. Their lives are transformed by a chance encounter with Bill (William Shatner) who may be the famous celebrity from Star Trek, but he is less than the heroic figure of the Final Frontier. In fact, the success Mark and Robert desire seems less attractive upon meeting Bill at a bookstore. He is reduced to wanting partners to make a one-man musical of Julius Caesar.
