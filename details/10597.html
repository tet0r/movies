Plot
“Swamp Thing” is a campy horror tale based on the comic book of the same name. When Doctor Alex Holland (Ray Wise) discovers a break through in his rejuvenation formula, the government sends Alice Cable (Adrienne Barbeau) to guard him from the clutches of of terrorist Anton Arcane (Louis Jourdan). Why no one realizes is Arcane’s men have infiltrated the base and when Holland refuses to give over the serum, they set him on fire and he runs out into the swamp. The bio chemicals in his formula have changed Holland into a Swamp Thing (Dick Durock). Swamp Thing and Cable travel the swamps avoiding capture from Arcane’s men but not before Arcane turns himself into a swamp like creature and Swamp Thing is forced to stop him.
