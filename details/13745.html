Plot
The movie is set in the future after the world has suffered a devastating war. Tradition tells that the two schools of martial arts, the Southern Cross and the North Star, must never fight. However, Lord Shin (Costas Mandylor) of Southern Cross kills the leader of the North Star Ryuken (Malcolm McDowell) to take over the control of the world. Kenshiro (Gary Daniels) is Ryuken’s son and a very skilled fighter. His destiny is to become the Fist of the North Star, but at first he is reluctant. He wanders the Waste Lands, avoiding the men of the Southern Cross. Lord Shin has also kidnapped Kenshiro’s love interest Juliet (Isako Washio) and left Kenshiro after almost killing him. With the guidance of his dead father Kenshiro seeks to defeat Lord Shin and become the Fist of the North Star.
