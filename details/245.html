Plot
In the Beginning of the movie we get to know how the four members of "The A Team" Col. John 'Hannibal' Smith(Liam Neeson), Lt. Templeton 'Faceman' Peck(Bradley Cooper), Cpl. Bosco 'B. A.' Baracus(Quinton Jackson), Murdock(Sharlto Copley) meet. The A Team goes on successful missions one after another for eight years. Then A Team which is in Iraq is sent on another mission to recover plates for printing 100 dollar bills. After doing the job while they are returning their commanding officer is killed, the plates are stolen and they are set up and are sent to different prison. Six months later CIA approaches Hannibal Smith and all four of them break out of prison to recover those plates so as to clear their names. The military is behind them all the time and finally they get the criminals caught. Even after they get the criminals caught they have problems with the military and they plan their final escape.
