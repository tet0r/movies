Plot
El Topo (Alejandro Jodorowsky) is dressed completely in black clothing and is a cowboy who wants revenge. He rides through a town with his son (Brontis Jodorowsky) and finds many slain people. He wants justice for them and also desires a girl, Mara (Mara Lorenzio); he ends up sacrificing his own son. He must also try to kill off a group of expert gunfighters. After being hurt, El Topo is nursed back to health by dwarfs, lame people, and other outcasts, and is reborn. He wants to dig out a tunnel from a cave so the lame ones can live in normal society. The town gets overrun by fanatically religious people, and villagers are forcefully branded with an icon.
