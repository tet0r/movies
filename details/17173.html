Plot
A shy professor of comparative literature at Monroe College in Chicago Clifford Skridlow’s (Dan Aykroyd) father (George Furth) runs the place. The college is financially-strapped. An encounter with four high-class prostitutes at a restaurant changes Clifford’s life forever. Smooth Walker (Howard Hesseman), the girls' pimp, is also in some financial trouble. A Chicago mob boss named Mom (Kate Murtagh) wants her money from Smooth Walker. Cliff ends up becoming Smooth Walker's right-hand man, and becomes Doctor Detroit. His dangerous mission is to protect the girls from Mom; all the while Clifford tries to hide his new life from his conservative parents. Also, he tries to help the college with a large endowment.
