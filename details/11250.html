Plot
George Herman Ruth (John Goodman) remains a big kid his entire life and is called The Babe. From his days as a wayward teenager in a home for incorrigibles with Brother Mathias (James Cromwell) where he learned how to play baseball to his early days with the Boston Red Sox and owner Harry Frazee (Peter Donat), Ruth has giant talent and grandiose taste. When Colonel Jack Ruppert (Bernard Kates) brings him to New York and the Yankees, he changes from a pitcher to a home run king. Along the way his appetites include marrying Claire Hodgson (Kelly McGillis) who sees his innate generosity and his excess make him beloved of fans when he promises a dying child he will hit a home run for him—and does so.
