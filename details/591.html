Plot
Ryu (Shin Ha-kyun) is a deaf mute. He works in a factory to help his sick sister who needs a kidney. When he is fired from the factory, Ryu talks to a black market dealer to sell him a kidney. Ryu gets a bank loan, but the organ dealers steal his money and one of his kidneys. Afterwards, the doctor finds a kidney for Ryu's sister, but the surgery money was stolen by the organ dealers. To get revenge and cash, Ryu and Yeong-mi (Bae Doona) plan to kidnap the daughter of a friend of the factory boss. Ryu's sister keeps her company while the factory arranges for a ransom. When Ryu's sister discovers his scheme to make money, she commits suicide to unburden him. Afterwards, Ryu kills the organ dealers and eats their kidneys. Yeong-mi is caught and tortured to death. Ryu swears revenge for his dead friend. Ryu is ambushed when he returns to his apartment by the person who killed his friend. Ryu is caught and drowned to death. Men from the terrorist organization that Yeong-mi was a part of, stab the man who killed Ryu and leave him dying in the sun.
