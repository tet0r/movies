Plot
Bumbling CIA agent Jack (Daniel Wu) is on a mission in Europe with a small teamed that is tasked with following a sultry female assassin who works for the mysterious Madame M. The team has a confrontation with the killer from which only Jack emerges unscathed, the assassin and the rest of Jack’s team perish. With her assassins demise Madame M seeks out new girls to train as viscous killers and finds them in the form of Katt (Anya) and Charlene (Maggie Q). Katt is a poor street girl who makes a living fighting grown men and Charlene is a daughter of wealthy parents, Charlene wants nothing more than to return to her privilege life after being kidnapped but Katt thinks her training under Madame M is better than a life on the street. All the recruits are well guarded and cannot leave and as time passes only Charlene, Katt and another trainee live to graduate Madame Ms training sessions. The film hints interestingly that the 2 girls may have developed a bond stronger than friendship, and one becomes romantic with the CIA agent that they are set on a collision course with, eventually the two factions must team up to bring down Madame M.
