Plot
“Jackass 3D” is the return of the group of crazy individuals who dare to do the most ridiculous and insane stunts. Johnny (Johnny Knoxville), Bam (Bam Margera), Ryan (Ryan Dunn), Wee-Man (Jason Acuña) and Steve-O (Steve-O) whip up a mixture of exciting and yet unthought-of stunts to the test. They prove that there is no Santa Clause and even he isn’t immune to their antics, Johnny outruns a rabid dog, proving that they can’t climb electric poles. Ryan stresses his eardrums as they mock the old Magnavox commercials and Wee Man uses a water ski on the asphalt of down town Los Angeles.
