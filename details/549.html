Plot
The Runaways were a 70’s all girl rock group featuring Joan Jett (Kristin Stewart) and Cherie Currie (Dakota Fanning). Their road to fame is documented from their teenage years as they go through the tough times with an abusive manager named Kim Fowley (Michael Shannon). The story is one that shows their persistence to succeed through the many tours and the spell of their manager when they finally find fame and shows how their persistence paid great dividends despite the rough times and roadblocks along the way. The rebellious California girls make good and become some the best known musicians and rock group in the country.
