Plot
Star Wars Clone Wars Volume 1 contains the first 20 three-minute episodes of the series that was shown on Cartoon Network. Clone Wars takes place between episodes 2 and 3 of the Star Wars movies and tells the story of the conflict between the republic and the independent systems that took place between those two films. In the episodes Count Dooku holds a competition to find the best bounty hunter by holding a tournament pitting the bounty hunters against each other. The bounty hunter that wins is then sent to kill Anakin Skywalker. Along with the main storyline other fights are shown, including Mace Windu, Obi-Wan Kenobi and Kit Fisto all battling different enemies.
