Plot
“Blown Away” is a film about a seventeen-year-old young woman named Megan (Nicole Eggert) who has just lost her mother. Megan’s father is a strict businessman and on a visit to her father’s ski resort, Megan falls for Rich (Corey Haim) an instructor at the resort. Now with a relationship that has become heavy, Rich learns that Megan has lost her mother in a car accident she blames her father for. As a matter of fact, Megan feels her father killed her mother and is now looking to have Rich help her kill her own father for revenge. Is Rich’s love that string that he’ll sit back and watch her kill her own father
