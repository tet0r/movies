Plot
Jeff Dunham, master ventriloquist and entertainer, stars as himself and his wacky crew of suitcase sidekicks in this live performance, taped at the Warner Theater in Washington, DC. Not only do the usual suspects appear, including Walter, Peanut, and Jose Jalapeño “on a steek”, new partners in crime appear. Melvin the Superhero, with his child like charm but sharp, adult wit and humor makes his debut, along with Achmed the Dead Terrorist, who's not quite convinced that he's dead. More than twice as long as the Comedy Central broadcast, “Spark of Insanity” will keep you laughing with its fast paced, extraordinary humor and bizarre talent of Jeff Dunham.
