Plot
Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull is the fourth installment of the action series created by George Lucas and directed by Steven Speilberg. The adventurous Indiana Jones (Harrison Ford) returns to his adventurous search for ancient artifacts and teams up with old acquaintance Marion Ravenwood (Karen Allen). The two characters, along new addition Mutt Williams (Shia LaBeouf), face new and sophisticated villains from the Soviet trying to find secrets regarding a crystal skull from ancient South American lore. Col. Dr. Irina Spalko (Cate Blanchett) leads her Soviet henchmen, along with the hesitant help of Indiana Jones, through the jungles of Peru for the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull. She has trouble however, since the Jones team will do whatever it takes safeguard and protect the jungle's treasures.
