Plot
“Hellraiser 6: Hellseeeker” sees the Kristy Cotton (Ashley Laurence) all grown up and married to Trevor Gooden (Dean Winters). The events of her past have dimmed a bit as her mind covers up what has happened in the past. When Kristy dies in a car crash, Trevor discovers the fateful Pandora’s box and his life becomes one of increasing odd events, seductive women, greed and murder, which makes him truly believe hell exists and he may be there, a personal hell of his own creation upon Kristy’s death. A path he follows till he finds the ever-mysterious Pinhead (Doug Bradley) who holds all the answers with in the mysterious box.
