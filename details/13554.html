Plot
Clark Kent (Adam Baldwin) is working on a Daily Planet article which sends him to Afghanistan. Meanwhile some workers from Lex Luthor's (James Martsers) company LexCorp die when they stumble on a spaceship when they're digging several miles below the Earth's surface. Unknowingly release a genetically engineered monster known as Doomsday. Doomsday escapes and begins attacking people as he closes in on Metropolis. The subplot involves Superman telling Lois Lane (Anne Heche) who he really is. Superman also must find the strength to destroy Doomsday but ultimately fails. The city of Metropolis is shocked and the police are unable to deal with Doomsday. Viewers are left wondering if Superman is dead or alive until the climax.
