Plot
Tenacious D: The Complete Masterworks is a compilation of the comic song writing duo, Tenacious D’s (Jack Black and Kyle Gass) HBO specials as well as a special life performance by the band London’s famous Brixton Academy in 2002. The compilation tells the story of the band from its inception all the way to their superstardom and world wide fame. The collection includes many of Tenacious D’s greatest hits in their music video format as well as performed live on stage in the concert footage. The band’s anthology also includes interviews from the band members and behind the scenes footage which is insightful and interesting for any Tenacious D fan.
