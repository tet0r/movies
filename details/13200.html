Plot
Video game tester Alex is evicted from his apartment, leaving him living at his grandma's house while he looks for a new place to live. His weed smoking provides most of the entertainment for the movie. Dante, Alex's drug dealer teaches a monkey karate to protect his "stuff". Alex and friends have a party at his grandmothers house after she drinks his pot. JP, the weirdo programmer, steals Alex's game and Alex's grandma has to prove its Alex's game.
