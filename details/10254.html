Plot
Gwendoline (Tawny Kitaen) is very beautiful and young. She takes her maid, Beth (Zabou Breitman), along with her in a crate, in which they ship themselves to China to try to find Gwendoline’s father, a professor, who has gone missing. Gwendoline runs into serious problems with the mafia in China, but then she is rescued by a perspiring sailor called Willard (Brent Huff). She offers him two thousand dollars to persuade him to help her to find her dad, who had last been spotted heading for the fabled land of Yik Yak in search of a butterfly species that is rare
