Plot
Teenager Dave Lizewski (Aaron Johnson) is a pretty normal teenager, who likes comic books and who doesn't have a lot of friends or any real strengths. Inspired by his favorite comic books, he decides one day to become his own superhero, who he names Kick Ass. While he has no superhero powers, he is determined to find a way to do good while being Kick Ass. He is joined by friends Red Mist (Christopher Mintz-Plassse), Hit Girl (Chloe Moretz), and Big Daddy (Nicolas Cage), and they all team together to fight the ills in their city, including drug dealers and criminals. Can this unlikely group of wannabe superheroes actually find a way to serve justice and protect their community?
