Plot
"Arang" is the harrowing tale about a haunted salt storehouse. Long ago, a broke ex-convict trying to make a new life for him, is slaughtered in the storehouse. Detective Min (So-yeong Choo) and Lee (Won-jung Jeong) a forensics officer, get assigned the case. Min begins having dreams, detailed visions of things that have happened in the salt storehouse. As two more deaths, related suspects occur, Min and Lee are brought to their main suspect, a doctor named So-young (Yun-ah Song). As they close in on So-Young, Min keeps seeing visions in the storehouse and begins to wonder if the sprits from beyond are trying to tell her something.
