Plot
This is a compilation of some of (Christopher Walken)'s best skits from Saturday Night Live. As one of Saturday Night Live's most memorable hosts, (Christopher Walken)'s skits became legendary classics. Perhaps his most famous skit, the "More Cowbell" skit, featuring the Blue Oyster Cult's "Don't Fear the Reaper", is included on this DVD, as well as "The Boulevard of Broken Balls" and his performances as the recurring ladies'-man in "The Continental". Other skits included that will have you laughing out loud are the euphemistic "Colonel Angus" and the mystifying "Trivial Psychic".
