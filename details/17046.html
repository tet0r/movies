Plot
Taking place in the distant future, Doom begins when communications are interrupted with an isolated scientific laboratory, Olduvai Research Station, located on Mars. Before communication broke, pieces of disturbing messages were transmitted; the only option is to send in the Rapid Response Tactical Squad. This team, lead by Sarge (played by The Rock) is deployed to neutralize the situation, but little do they know an experiment gone horribly wrong literally unleashed Hell. As the laboratory becomes a battle ground, the team must seal off the portal to earth, before the whole planet suffers the same fate as Olduvai. The marines are in for the fight of their life as they battle the demons and nightmarish creatures that resided in Hell’s domain in this special effects loaded Sci-fi adventure that blends action and suspense.
