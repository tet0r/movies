Plot
Seasons 1 & 2 of Family Guy introduces us to the Griffin family. Peter Griffin, the big lummox, who has no control over his mouth or his stupidity. Lois Griffin, the intelligent woman behind the man, who gave up a life of wealth and luxury to marry for love. Meg Griffin, who is constantly embarrassed by her family, and the fact that she's plain and ordinary. Chris Griffin, who's heart is bigger than his mind. Stewie Griffin, the homicidal infant with delusions of matricide. Brian Griffin, the talking, drinking, wisecracking dog.
