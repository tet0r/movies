Plot
A singer, Mima (Iwao Junko), has just decided to leave music to be an actress. This makes her fans angry, including one, Me-Mania, who starts stalking her. Mima gets a starring role in a drama series. However, they want her to do a rape scene, and Mima's manager, Rumi (Matsumoto Rika), tells her this will ruin her reputation. Mima goes ahead anyway, but becomes so upset by the scene that she begins to have flashbacks and hallucinations. This is exacerbated by finding a website, "Mima's Room", that carries diary entries supposedly written by Mima herself, entries she doesn't remember writing. Then people around her start getting killed and she believes she may have done it. It was actually Me-Mania, who then attempts to rape Mima and is killed in self-defense. Mima then discovers that Rumi is in fact the author of "Mima's Room" and has incited Me-Mania to kill the people who Rumi is convinced have forced Mima into degrading work. Rumi believes she herself is the "true" Mima, because Mima is no longer worthy of her own image. Mima flees and Rumi pursues her, but Mima manages to incapacitate her, and Rumi is institutionalized.
