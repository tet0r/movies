Plot
Ash (Malgorzata Foremniak) lives alone in a city in ruins. The only pleasure she finds in life is to play the game Avalon. The game provides escape from a dismal world. But there is danger in playing the game. It causes people to go crazy. Ash is determined to beat the game and go to the next level. As the game takes her deeper into an unreal world, she struggles to battle the enemy and yet be able to return to reality where he life means nothing to her since her loved ones have all gone insane from the game. But she knows that her pet depends on her for food, so she must succeed and return to reality.
