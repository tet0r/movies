Plot
On a futuristic Asian island that is ruled by an oppressive, harsh monarchy, rebel fighters seek to remove from power the oppressive ruling government. Named the House of Takemikazuchi, a band of the deadliest assassins the country has to offer, is hired by the government to enforce the laws and suppress the rebels. A conspiracy involving a past murder is the warning sign for the regime’s downfall. The clan's most known and brutal fighter is a woman; Princess Yuki (Yumiko Shaku). She manages to befriend one of the rebel fighters and then decides to seek revenge against Byakurai (Kyusaku Shimada) the mercenaries’ leader.
