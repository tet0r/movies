Plot
The world of 2039 is a battered world, one that is no longer lead by governments, but rather by large companies. Tekken Corporation is in control of North America, and the head of Tekken, Heihachi Mishima (Cary-Hiroyuki Tagawa), has decided to run a fighting tournament dubbed the King of the Iron Fist. The tournament is meant to be a distraction to the unhappy masses under Tekken's control. When Jin Kazama (John Foo), a teenage street fighter and petty criminal, watches his mother die he is determined to enter the contest. Once he gains entry into the contest the real story of his life unfolds as he discovers that Kazuya Mishima (Ian Anthony Dale), son of Heihachi, is his real father and that he could possibly become the heir to the Tekken empire. In order to survive the tournament, however, and reach the status of CEO of Tekken Jin must enlist the aid of friends Christie Monteiro (Kelly Overton) and Steve Fox (Luke Goss) among others.
