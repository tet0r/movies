Plot
A Las Vegas performer who made it big as a magician and became very friendly with the mob decides to turn states evidence. Buddy Israel (Jeremy Piven) decides to testify against the mob and everyone that ever had dealings with him seem to have ordered a hit on him. The million dollar hit is overheard by FBI investigators at a mob home and the FBI desperately tries to get to him first. Buddy is hidden in a hotels luxury suite where the FBI think that is the last place that would be assassins will look for him. Assassins of every description are after him to get the money. A dark hidden secret comes to light at the end.
