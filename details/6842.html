Plot
When God decides that the human race is no longer worthy of Him, things are in dire straights for everyone on Earth. He sends a legion of angels to bring on the Apocalypse, giving the world little hope of survival. The one flicker of hope comes in a random group of diners, trapped together in an out of the way diner in the middle of nowhere along with Archangel Michael (Paul Bettany). With the fate of the world in their hands, it's up to this group of strangers to find a way to pull together and save the world from the Apocalypse.
