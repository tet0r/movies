Plot
In an alternate 1985, real heroes had come and gone since the 1940's, and re-emerge with the murder of on of their founding members, The Comedian (Jeffrey Dean Morgan). Investigating the murder, nihilistic hero Rorschach (Jackie Earle Haley) stumbles upon a much larger plot involving his ex-compatriots and the world on the brink of global destruction.
