Plot
“Godzilla: Final Wars” is the fiftieth anniversary film celebrating over fifty years of the Tokyo stomping monster. In this latest addition to the film series, The Earth Defense Force has declared Godzilla (Tsutomu Kitagawa) their most unbeatable foe. So they trap him in the artic under tons of ice. With the threat of Godzilla out of the way, various monsters begin to invade Earth. Anguirus (Toshihiro Ogura), Ebirah (Toshihiro Ogura), Hedorah (Toshihiro Ogura), Kamacuras, (Motokuni Nakagawa) King Caesar (Motokuni Nakagawa), Kumonga (Naoko Kamio), Rodan (Naoko Kamio), and Zilla (Naoko Kamio) appear simultaneously and the Earth Defense Force can’t cope. With the appearance of the culprit who makes himself known as the aliens called Xilians. They are led by The Controller of Planet X (Kazuki Kitamura) who will unleash the menace of Monster X (Kenji Kohashi) on the world unless Godzilla can stop him. This is it, the final showdown to end all showdowns!
