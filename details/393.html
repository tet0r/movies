Plot
“Love Ranch” is the real life tale of a married couple, Charlie Bontempo (Joe Pesci) and Grace Bontempo (Helen Mirren) who in the mid-70 opened the first legal brothel in Nevada. Of course to have an active brothel, women are needed as Charlie and Grace set out to recruit their first girls in the way of; Mallory (Taryn Manning), Irene (Gina Gershon), Christina (Scout Taylor-Compton), Samantha (Ling Bai) and Alana (Elise Neal). Everything is going well save for when Grace is diagnosed with cancer. Now in order to raise the money to save his wife, Charlie takes on an Argentine boxer named Armando Bruza (Sergio Peris-Mencheta). Charlie has not only the ranch to explain to the IRS but also this cash prize boxer and his new-found income.
