Plot
After war has destroyed the world as we know it, the surviving humans band together to use technology to create cyborgs, man-like robots. These cyborgs are built with lasting loyalty to their human master. They have been programmed to feel which is a monumental step in technology that men believe will be advantageous. However, this feeling that the cyborgs have is the undoing of the human race. Their feelings have led the cyborgs to desire freedom. A rebellion is brewing and only the military leaders of R (Yoo Ji-tae) and Noma (Yoon Chan) have the ability to stop this before it becomes another all out war. And this time the humans may not survive.
