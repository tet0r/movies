Plot
“The Batman/ Superman Movie” is the first pairing of the Dark Knight, Batman (Kevin Conroy) and Krypton’s last son, Superman (Tim Daly) in the Paul Dini animated universe. The Joker (Mark Hamill) who is looking for some amusement has moved on to Metropolis in search of Lex Luthor (Clancy Brown). The Joker has a proposition for the business tycoon, Joker has a jade statue of a dragon that just happens to be made out of Kryptonite. The downside is, coming to Metropolis has also meant Batman has followed the Joker. Now you have Batman and Superman working together, the Joker and Luthor at each other’s throats and Bruce Wayne (Kevin Conroy) dating Lois Lane (Dana Delany) with a confused Clark Kent (Tim Daly) caught in the middle. Then again, this is why they’re called The World’s Finest.
