Plot
Mute Hee-Jin (Suh Jung) is caretaker for a fleet of houseboats and provides them with fishing tackle and occasional sexual services when they hire the houseboats. Hyun-Shik (Kim Yoo-Suk) hires one of the houseboats but seems interested neither in fishing now in sex. He is on run from police. He once attempts suicide but Jin saves him. They develop a silent relationship. At first she spurns his sexual advances and arranges for a prostitute to visit him but later agrees. He attempts suicide again when he panics due to the police coming there. Then she tends to him and both fall in love leading to further jealousy and violence.
