Plot
Dr. Chiba Atsuko (Megumi Hayashibara) is one of a team of scientists who have invented a device that allows the wearer to enter the dreams of other people. Under the persona Paprika, she uses the tool to help investigate the psychological problems of others while they are sleeping. But when one of the devices is stolen and used to cause coma-inducing nightmares in others, it is up to her and her other self to track down the thief before further harm takes place.
