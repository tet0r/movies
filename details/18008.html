Plot
Fans of the original documentary Reefer Madness and anyone who enjoys black comedy and political lampooning will enjoy this musical satire. Reefer Madness: The Musical takes a tongue in cheek look at both the scare tactics used in the early years of Public Safety Announcements, as well as the attitudes and beliefs held currently. Nothing is sacred in this film adaption of the stage musical, from Jesus to racism, high school sweethearts and apple pie, everything is fair game.
