Plot
Critters 3: You Are What They Eat is the third installment in the Critters series of movies. In this film the Critters return to Earth and this time reek havoc on the residents of a Los Angeles apartment complex. The Critters terrorize the residents and claim many of their lives in their quest for sustenance and global destruction. The apartment residents’ only chance for survival is to make it up to the building’s roof and wait out the Critters’ onslaught until help can arrive and exterminate the threat. Help is on the way in the form of bounty hunter and Critter survivor, Charlie McFadden (Don Keith Opper) but can he help the residents before they are consumed by the Critters.
