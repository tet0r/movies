Plot
“Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles 3” sees our four favorite ninja turtles, Leonardo (Mark Caso), Donatello (Jim Raposa), Michelangelo (David Fraser) and Raphael (Matt Hill) are accidentally transported back in time to feudal Japan. It’s here that they will learn the most valuable lesson of their Master Splinter (James Murray) of becoming one. With the aid of western weapons, Lord Norinaga (Sab Shimono) has spread fear and tyranny across the countryside. When the turtles stumble upon a village besieged by oppression it is here they are told of the legend of the four green warriors who will defeat Lord Norinaga. Meanwhile, Casey Jones (Elias Koteas) and April O’Neil (Paige Turco) back in the present day must entertain four honor guards who have switched places with the turtles.
