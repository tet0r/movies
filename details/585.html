Plot
An Amazon beauty princess named Diana (Keri Russell) is raised on a mystical island called Themyscira. The gods and goddesses of Olympus have granted Diana with a host of incredible strength of super human powers that are unparalleled to any ordinary human. When a fighter pilot named Steve Trevor (Nathan Fillion) crashes on the island, Diana is forced to bring Trevor back to civilization and at the same time take down Ares, the God of war as he is determined to destroy both her people as well as the world. It is time for Diana to make use of her gift of unimaginable powers to become Wonder Woman to save mankind from the evil Ares.
