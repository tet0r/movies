Plot
"Death Race 2" is the story of the original mad racer, Frankenstein (Luke Goss) and how he came to rise as a legend in the future penal system. When Lucas (Luke Goss) goes to jail for a crime he did not commit, Warden Parks (Patrick Lyster) wishes to use him in a new idea for death racing; using inmates to race to the death for viewing figures as a way to make money for the prison system. Teaming with September Jones (Lauren Cohan) and Markus Kane (Sean Bean), Lucas under the guise of Frankenstein must take on the likes of Goldberg (Danny Trejo) and Rocco (Joe Vaz) in what can only be a new meaning to race to the death.
