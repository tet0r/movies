Plot
Three cops join forces in order to fight the Ronin Gang. Chan Chun (Nicholas Tse) vows revenge on the gang after his finacee is killed in a robbery the gang takes credit for. Trainee Inspector Fong Yik-Wei (Shawn Yue) is after the Ronin Gang after too many of his fellow officers are killed by them. Wai King-Ho (Jaycee Chan) is after the rescue of his brother who has gone undercover and missing within the gang. All three men have completely different motives for pursuing the Ronin Gang but none are any less willing to do whatever it takes to track down the ruthless killers.
