Plot
Cousins Bo and Luke Duke raise mischief in a ’69 Dodge Charger named the General Lee. When they’re not getting into trouble with Sheriff Roscoe Coltrane for their crazy schemes, the two young men run a Moonshine business with Uncle Jesse and their gorgeous cousin Daisy Dukes. After Boss Hogg, a crooked local politician, evicts the Dukes from their farm, the two troublemakers seek the help from Katie and Annette to uncover Hogg’s malicious plot.
