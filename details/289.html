Plot
In this sequel to Dawn of the Dead, the world has essentially been taken over by the undead and above ground zombies rule the day. The last hope of civilization lies in the few surviving scientists and a remnant of the military, some of whom are hunkered down in an underground bunker in Florida trying to make plans to take back their world. Chief scientist Logan (Richard Liberty) and military man Captain Rhodes (Joe Pilato) are partners by circumstance, not choice, and their antagonism to each other almost destroys any hope of success. The arrival of another scientist Sara (Lori Cardille) and some others defuses what could have been a fatal confrontation. Rhodes and his men are set to the task of capturing zombies so that the scientists can study them with the hope of a scientific solution, while Rhodes and the other soldiers would prefer a way to simply blast them all off the face of the earth. The human interactions play out against the struggle to find a way to save the world, with personal frictions and even a love interest along the way.
