Plot
Retired Black Ops Agent Frank Moses (Bruce Willis) is enjoying the quiet retired life until he is drawn out of retirement by a high-tech assassin, William Cooper (Karl Urban). Setting out to save himself, Moses must also save his past allies from his Black Ops days. He eventually tracks down Joe Matheson (Morgan Freeman), Marvin Boggs (John Malkovich), Gabriel Singer (James Remar) and Ivan Simanov (Brian Cox) who all have a stake in this ordeal. With his team reassembled with some new added faces, Frank sets out to stop his would be assassins at their own game.
