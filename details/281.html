Plot
Critters 4: They’re Invading Your Space Critters 4: They’re Invading Your Space is the fourth installment in the Critters series of horrifying, yet darkly comedic movies. This episode takes place just after bounty hunter Charlie McFadden (Don Keith Opper) has the opportunity to kill the last two known specimen of the Critters race but must halt his intentions as it would be illegal for him to completely exterminate the race. He takes the last eggs with him, but becomes lost in space and cryogenically frozen for fifty three years. He is found by a wreckage collection team led by the unscrupulous Rick (Anders Hove), who opens the capsule and releases the Critters. Charlie must again hunt down the Critters before they consume all humans on the space station.
