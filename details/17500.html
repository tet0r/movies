Plot
When Tenshu (Hideo Sakaki) is convicted of murder because he killed the man who raped his girlfriend, he is sentenced to the electric chair to die. But, because of some weird quirk, Tenshu does not die. This poses quite a dilemma for the officials. So, they give him a choice. He can either resubmit himself to the electric chair or agree to be a subject in a secret experiment. It doesn’t take Tenshu long to make up his mind. He decides to go the route of being a test subject. Once he realizes what he is now a part of, will Tenshu regret his choice?
