Plot
In Space Prison, the Critters are sent to be transported to another station. However, their spaceship is hijacked, and two transforming bounty hunters are on their trail. In Kansas, the Brown family are the unlucky recipients of the Critters. The space ship crash lands, and Jay Brown (Billy Green Bush), the father, and Brad Brown (Scott Grimes), the son, go to investigate. The Critters discuss amongst themselves and decide that they want food. The Critters attack the Brown family, and Brad goes for help. He brings back the Bounty Hunters. The Critters, driven back to their ship, kidnap April. She is freed shortly after, and the ship is booby trapped with a firecracker. The Critters try to escape, but after destroying the Brown’s house, their ship explodes. The Bounty Hunters fix the Brown’s house and leave, while the camera pans to green eggs laid in the barn.
