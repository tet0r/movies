Plot
Kakihara (Tadanobu Asano), an obsessive sadomasochist yakuza, is searching for Anjo, his missing boss. He went missing with 300 million yen, but Kakihara just seems to want him back. Meanwhile, a violent martial artists with knives in his feet named Ichi (Nao Omori) is killing Anjo's henchman one by one in spectacularly bloody fashion. Kakihara and his gang follow a series of leads that directs them towards Ichi and his mysterious controller, Jijii (Shinya Tsukamoto), and a Tokyo rooftop showdown.
