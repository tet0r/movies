Plot
Battlestar Galactica: Razor is a made-for-TV movie set in the re-imagined Battlestar Galactica universe, which originally aired on the SciFi Network on November 24, 2007. Razor follows the actions under the Battlestar Pegasus through several key points, including the attack on the Colonies by the Cylons, a disastrous counter-attack, a betrayal of a trusted associate, and the forced drafting of civilians, all alluded to in previous episodes. Events are primarily viewed from a focus on young officer Kendra Shaw (Stephanie Chaves-Jacobsen), as she deals with the destruction of the Colonies, becomes a "razor" under the guidance of Admiral Cain (Michelle Forbes), and leads a critical mission as first officer under Commander Lee Adama (Jamie Bamber).
